About Me

I am currently pursuing a degree in Electrical Engineering at Pulchowk Campus. Alongside my technical pursuits, I serve as a Graphics Designer for college programs, creating banners and visuals to enhance event appeal. This dual role reflects my ability to blend analytical thinking with creative expression, bridging engineering and design.

Proficient in Microsoft Excel and Word, I excel in data management and document creation. My critical thinking skills enable effective troubleshooting and strategic decision-making, while my creativity shines in graphic design and animation.

With two years of experience as a Computer Operator at Trinity International College, I managed examinations, coordinated logistics, and designed certificates and IDs. This role honed my technical, organizational, and creative skills, preparing me for diverse challenges in both administrative and design-oriented roles.
